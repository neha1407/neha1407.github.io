Chinal is a harmonic script typeface, drawn and created by Måns Grebäck during 2017. 
This high quality typeface family comes in four complementing weights: 
Chinal Light, Chinal Medium, Chinal Bold and Chinal Black.

The font also contains multiple extra OpenType features; including 
contextual alternates, a set of stylistic alternate characters, 
swashes, initial and final letters, ornaments and ligatures. 

With its hundreds of glyphs, Chinal supports a very wide range of languages.
